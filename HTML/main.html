Over time, my curiosity about how these immersive experiences are created turned into a desire to create them. I love the process of designing and programming games - the ability to create something that can provide others with as much joy as I have derived from playing is incredibly satisfying for me. Games are not just fun, they are also art and a way to express yourself.
